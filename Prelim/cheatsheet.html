HTML Semantic Tags
Common Semantic Tags
<header>Top section of page or section. Logo, nav, title.
<nav>Navigation links block.
<main>Primary content of the page. Only one per page.
<section>A thematic group of content. Has a heading.
<article>Self-contained content (blog post, news item).
<aside>Sidebar or related content beside main content.
<footer>Bottom section. Credits, links, contact.
<figure>Wraps images/diagrams with optional caption.
<figcaption>Caption inside a <figure>.
